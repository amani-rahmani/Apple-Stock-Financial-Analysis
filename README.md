# Apple-Stock-Financial-Analysis
This project analyzes Apple Inc. (AAPL) historical stock data to understand its price trends, volatility, and overall financial performance over time. Using Python, I performed exploratory data analysis (EDA), calculated key financial metrics (KPIs).
## Business Problem

* How has Apple stock performed historically, and what can we learn about its risk, volatility, and long-term returns?

* This analysis helps answer:

* Is Apple’s stock trending upward over time?

* How volatile is Apple on a day-to-day basis?

* What level of risk would an investor have experienced?

* How strong is Apple’s long-term growth?
##  Data Source

* Source: Yahoo Finance

* Ticker: AAPL (Apple Inc.)

* Time Period: Last 5 years
##  Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The following analyses were performed:

### Closing Price Trend

* Visualized Apple’s stock price over time

* Added 20-day and 50-day moving averages to identify trends

### Daily Return Distribution

* Analyzed day-to-day price changes

* Used a histogram to understand volatility and extreme movements

### Rolling Volatility

* Calculated 20-day rolling volatility

* Identified periods of higher market risk

### Cumulative Return

* Measured long-term growth of a hypothetical investment
## Key Insights

* Apple stock shows a strong long-term upward trend, despite short-term fluctuations.

* Daily returns are mostly small, but occasional large movements contribute to volatility.

* Periods of high volatility align with broader market uncertainty.

* Long-term cumulative returns indicate significant growth, making Apple attractive for long-term investors.

* Maximum drawdown highlights that investors must tolerate temporary losses during market downturns.
